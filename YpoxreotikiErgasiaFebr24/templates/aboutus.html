Planet. Climate.

At DS Airlines, we care about the impact of every flight. That’s why we’re driving urgent action towards net zero emissions. We have a long history of managing and reducing our carbon emissions and a clear roadmap to achieving net zero carbon emissions by 2050, with ambitious short, medium and long-term initiatives to achieve our goals.

More than a flight. More than an experience.

The DS Airlines Experience is more than a flight. It’s about making every single journey as special and original as you are, whatever your reason to travel. We’re always here to help you feel relaxed and secure from the airport to boarding and beyond.

Partners and alliances. Different brands, same outstanding service.

We think you should be able to make all your travel plans in one place, and not have to worry about things like connections and timetables. That's why we have arrangements with a selection of other airlines, to extend the number of destinations we offer and enhance the benefits to you. You can book it all right here on dsairlines.com.
